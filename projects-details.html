Health & Wellness - University Medical Center

General Objective

Guarantee the health and well-being of students on campus.

Proposed Facility

Creation of a functional university medical center, including:

  • A qualified health professional
  • First aid and rest area
  • Basic pharmacy
  • Rapid intervention protocol

Complementary Actions

  • Awareness on hygiene and prevention
  • Basic health monitoring
  • Referral to external medical structures if necessary

Expected Impact

  • Reduction of health risks
  • Improved student comfort and safety

Governance, Transparency & Audit Control

General Objective

Guarantee sound, transparent and credible management of student funds.

Proposed Mechanism

Implementation of an internal audit control mechanism, including:

  • Strict tracking of revenues and expenses
  • Periodic audit of funds
  • Publication of accessible financial reports to students
  • Creation of an Audit and Transparency Commission

Resources Involved

  • Student contributions
  • Revenue from services (events, activities)
  • Partnerships and external support

Expected Impact

  • Student confidence
  • Credibility with administration
  • Responsible and sustainable governance
